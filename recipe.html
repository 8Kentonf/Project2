Kenton Fong's Prefered Biscuit Recipe

This is one of my go to recipe if I want some easy to make tasty biscuits

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1 Tbsp Baking Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Sugar
  • 6 Tbsp Cold Salted Butter
  • 3/4 Cup Milk

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425F, line a cookie sheet with parchment paper
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, sugar in a bowl
  3. Use a box grater to grate the butter into the dry mixture
  4. Mix and add the Milk, Mix until it just comes together
  5. Turn dough out onto floured workspace and flatten into a square
  6. Cut into squares and bake for 12 mins
